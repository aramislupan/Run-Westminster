We run various events to suit your fitness level, come and join us!

half marathon

The Victoria Half Marathon course follows the full Marathon route, taking in both Vincent Street and Dolphin square along this historic route.

Supersonic 10k

The Supersonic 10K takes place on Sunday morning and is a fantastic flat and fast course, great for setting a quick time. The Supersonic 10K is an increasingly popular race distance, a great step-up for those who have completed a 5K in the past, but aren’t quite ready for the half marathon distance.

Supersonic 5k

The Supersonic 5K takes place on Saturday early afternoon and is a fantastic flat and fast course, great for setting a quick time. The event starts on Page street and finishes near Westminster Cathedral in Victoria Street.

Supernova 5k

The Supernova 5K starts on Vincent Square and finishes near Parliament Square. This beautiful route takes in through Schomberg House and takes place at dusk with an emphasis on light around the route.
